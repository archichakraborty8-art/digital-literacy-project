# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1. Do not click on suspicious email links.  
2. Verify website URLs before entering sensitive information.  
3. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.  
4. Enable two-factor authentication for online accounts.  
5. Regularly update devices and antivirus software.  
6. Avoid sharing personal or financial details on unknown platforms.  
7. For online payments, double-check UPI/transaction requests before approving.  
8. Do not save payment credentials on public devices.
